  • The declining balance depreciation. This method assumes the rate of depreciation is proportional to the current value, with the initial rate of depreciation twice the straight-line rate, with depreciation stopping when the value is the scrap value. We let N be the estimated life of the asset and we let the rate of depreciation be:

    \begin{equation*} Depreciation\ rate=1- \sqrt[N]{ \frac{residual\ value}{cost\ of\ fixed\ asset}}\end{equation*}
