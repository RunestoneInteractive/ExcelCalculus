Item.

The sum of digits method of depreciation. It assumes the rate of depreciation is proportional to the expected remaining useful life of the piece of equipment. For example with a sum of years method and defining V(t) as the value, EL is the expected lifetime, k is a constant, and t is time, we would have:

\begin{equation*} V' (t)= -k (EL-t) \end{equation*}
