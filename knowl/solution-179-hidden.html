We want to find \(\frac{dz}{dy}\text{.}\) We know \(\frac{dz}{dx}\) and we can compute \(\frac{dy}{dx}\text{,}\) hence we compute:

\begin{equation*} \frac{dy}{dx}=2x-5,\text{ hence at }x=7\text{ we have }\frac{dy}{dx}=9 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \frac{dz}{dy}=\frac{dz/dx}{dy/dx}=\frac{8}{9} \end{equation*}
