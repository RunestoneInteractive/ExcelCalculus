The anti-derivative is:

\begin{equation*} F(x)=\frac{100 (0.95)^x }{\ln(0.95)} +C \end{equation*}

\(F(5)= 9\) implies that

\begin{equation*} F(5)=\frac{100 (0.95^5 )}{\ln(0.95)} +C=9 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} C=9-\frac{100 (0.95^5 )}{\ln(0.95)}\approx 9+1508.54=1517.54 \end{equation*}

Then

\begin{equation*} F(x)\approx\frac{100 (0.95^x )}{\ln(0.95)} +1517.54 \end{equation*}
