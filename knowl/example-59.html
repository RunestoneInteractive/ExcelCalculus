Example 4.2.10. General derivatives of quotients.

Find the derivatives of the following functions:

  1. \(f(x)=((6x^2+100))/(x^3+x).\)
  2. \(g(x)=(1.07)^x/(3x+5).\)

Solution:

  1. \begin{equation*} f'(x)=\left(\frac{6x^2+100}{x^3+3}\right)' =\frac{(12x)(x^3+3)-(6x^2+100)(3x^2+1)}{(x^3+2)^2} \end{equation*}
  2. \begin{equation*} g'(x)=\left(\frac{1.07^x}{3x+5}\right)' =\frac{(\ln(1.07)1.07^x)(3x+5)-(1.07^x)(3)} {(3x+5)^2}. \end{equation*}
