Solution 4.2.4.13.1.

Quotient Rule:

\begin{equation*} f'(x)=\frac{(\ln(x))'(2x+7)-(\ln(x))(2x+7)'}{(2x+7)^2} =\frac{(1/x)(2x+7)-(\ln(x))(2)}{(2x+7)^2} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} =\frac{x}{x}\frac{ (1/x)(2x+7)-(\ln(x))(2)}{(2x+7)^2} = \frac{2x+7-2x \ln(x)}{x (2x+7)^2}. \end{equation*}
