Solution 7.3.1.9.1.

The anti-derivative is: \(F(x)=(3x^2)/2+5 \ln|x|\)

\begin{equation*} \int_2^{10} 3x+\frac{5}{x} dx =F(10)-F(2) =\frac{300}{2}+5 \ln(10)-\left(\frac{12}{2}+5 \ln(2) \right) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} =150+5 \ln(10)-6-5 \ln(2)=144+5(\ln(10)-\ln(2) ) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} = 144+5 \ln\left(\frac{10}{2}\right)=144+5 \ln(5) \end{equation*}
