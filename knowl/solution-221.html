Solution 7.2.3.1.
  1. \begin{equation*} F' (x)=\frac{d}{dx} [\ln(x^5 )+ 9]=\frac{1}{x^5} (5x^4 )+ 0=\frac{5}{x} \end{equation*}

    or

    \begin{equation*} F' (x)=\frac{d}{dx} [\ln(x^5 )+ 9]=\frac{d}{dx} [5\ln(x)+ 9]=5\frac{1}{x}+ 0=\frac{5}{x} \end{equation*}
  2. \begin{equation*} \int_1^{20}\frac{5}{x} dx=F(20)-F(1)=\ln(20^5 )+9-(\ln(1)+9)=ln(20^5) =14.98 \end{equation*}

  3. The midpoint sum with N = 200 gives an approximation of 14.978
