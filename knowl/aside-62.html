Definition: Given a probability density function, \(f(x)\) , the related Cumulative Distribution Function, \(CDFf(x)\), is a function that measures how much area is over the interval \((-\infty,x]\).

In calculus terms, \(CDFf(x)\), the Cumulative Distribution Function of \(f(x)\), is \((\int_{-\infty}^x f(t)dt\).
