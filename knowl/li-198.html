Item 2.2.1.1.c.

Evaluate cost, demand price, revenue, and profit at \(q_0\text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} cost(6)=3 (6)+10=28 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} demand price(6)=-2 (6)+20=8 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} Revenue(6)=-2 6^2 +20 (6)=-72+120=48 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} Profit=revenue-cost =48-28=20 \end{equation*}
