Item 6.2.7.a.

Give the 2 functions of one variable through \(P_1\) obtained by holding each variable constant.

\begin{equation*} Production(300,C)=10 300^{0.74} C^{0.26}=680.879 C^{0.26} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} Production(L,30)=10 L^{0.74} 30^{0.26}=24.2132 L^{0.74} \end{equation*}

Hence both cross sections are exponential function.
