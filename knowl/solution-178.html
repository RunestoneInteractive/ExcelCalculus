Solution 5.2.9.1.

We want to find \(\frac{dz}{dx}\text{.}\) We know \(\frac{dz}{dy}\) and we can compute \(\frac{dy}{dx}\text{,}\) hence we compute:

\begin{equation*} \frac{dy}{dx}=2x-3,\text{ hence at }x=7\text{ we have }\frac{dy}{dx}=11 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \frac{dz}{dx}=\frac{dz}{dy}\frac{dy}{dx}=(3)(11)=33 \end{equation*}
