Solution 4.3.1.5.1.
\begin{equation*} m'(x)=\frac{1}{\ln(\ln(x^2+3))} \frac{d}{dx} \ln(\ln(x^2+3) ) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} =\frac{1}{\ln(\ln(x^2+3))}* \frac{1}{\ln(x^2+3)}*\frac{d}{dx} \ln(x^2+3) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} =\frac{1}{\ln(\ln(x^2+3))}* \frac{1}{\ln(x^2+3)}*\frac{1}{x^2+3}*\frac{d}{dx} (x^2+3) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} =\frac{1}{\ln(\ln(x^2+3))}* \frac{1}{\ln(x^2+3)}*\frac{1}{x^2+3}*2x \end{equation*}
