Item 7.8.1.7.a.

The two curve intersect at the point of market equilibrium, \((70, 100)\text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} ProdSurplus= \int_0^{70} (100)- (30+q)\ dx=2450. \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} ConsSurplus= \int_0^{70} (170-q)-100\ dx=2450. \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} TotalSocialGain=ProdSurplus+ConsSurplus=4900. \end{equation*}
