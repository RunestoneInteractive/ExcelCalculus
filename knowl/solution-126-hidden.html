Quotient Rule:

\begin{equation*} m'(x)=\frac{(x^2-5x)' (x^2+3)-(x^2-5x) (x^2+3)'}{(x^2+3)^2} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} =\frac{(2x+5)(x^2+3)-(x^2-5x)(2x)}{(x^2+3)^2}. \end{equation*}

We can simplify this :

\begin{equation*} m'(x)=\frac{2x^3+5x^2+6x+15-(2x^3-10x^2 )}{(x^2+3)^2} =\frac{-5x^2+6x+15}{(x^2+3)^2}. \end{equation*}
