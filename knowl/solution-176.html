Solution 5.2.5.1.

  • If \(y=3x+5\) then \(x= \frac{y-5}{3}\text{.}\) Hence \(z=4x+7=\frac{4}{3} (y-5)+7=\frac{4}{3} y+\frac{1}{3}\text{.}\) So \(\frac{dz}{dy}=\frac{4}{3}\text{.}\)

  • \begin{equation*} \frac{dz}{dx}=4, \quad \frac{dy}{dx}=3,\quad \frac{dz}{dy}= \frac{dz/dx}{dy/dx}=\frac{4}{3} \end{equation*}
