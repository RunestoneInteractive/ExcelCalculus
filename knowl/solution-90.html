Solution 3.3.3.1.
\begin{equation*} f(x)\approx f(20)+mf(21)*(x-20) \end{equation*}

Note that we have to compute \(mf(21)\)

\begin{equation*} mf(21)=f(21)-f(20)= 210-200=10 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} f(x)\approx 200+10*(x-20) \end{equation*}

Next we evaluate the linear approximation at 28:

\begin{equation*} f(28)\approx 200+10*(28-20)=200+80=280 \end{equation*}
