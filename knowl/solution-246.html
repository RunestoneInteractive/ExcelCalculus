Solution 7.4.1.17.1.

Let \(u=3x^2+7\text{.}\) Then \(du=6x\ dx\text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} \int_1^6 x(3x^2+7)^{1/2} dx=\frac{1}{6}\int_{u=10}^{u=115} u^{1/2} \ du \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} =\left.\frac{1}{9}u^{3/2}\right|_{u=10}^{u=115} =\frac{1}{9} (115^{3/2}-10^{3/2})\approx 133.51 \end{equation*}
