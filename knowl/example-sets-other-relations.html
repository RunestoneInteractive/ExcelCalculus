Example5.2.4

Other relations between sets often hold true. For example, \begin{equation*}( A \setminus B) \cap (B \setminus A) = \emptyset.\end{equation*} To see that this is true, observe that\begin{align*} ( A \setminus B) \cap (B \setminus A) & = ( A \cap B') \cap (B \cap A')\\ & = A \cap A' \cap B \cap B'\\ & = \emptyset. \end{align*}
