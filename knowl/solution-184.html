Solution 5.3.1.3.1.
  • \begin{equation*} price=50-\frac{q}{100}=50-\frac{2500}{100}=50-25=\$25 \end{equation*}
  • \begin{equation*} E=\frac{-d Q}{d P}*\frac{P(Q_0 )}{Q_0} = \frac{-1}{dP/dQ} \frac{P(Q_0 )}{Q_0} = \frac{-1}{-1/100} \frac{25}{2500}=\frac{2500}{2500}=1 \end{equation*}

  • When \(E = 1\) we have unit elasticity and the revenue is at a maximum. Hence we want to leave the price as is.

  • The maximum revenue occurs when \(E = 1\text{,}\) so we want \(q = 2500\) and \(P = \$25\text{.}\)
