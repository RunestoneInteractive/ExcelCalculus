Item 6.2.1.9.a.

Give the 3 functions of one variable through \(P_1\) obtained by holding each variable constant.

\begin{equation*} f(x,3,-1)=\frac{x+9+2}{x^2+9+1}=\frac{x+11}{x^2+10} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} f(2,y,-1)=\frac{2+3y+2}{4+y^2+1}=\frac{3y+4}{y^2+5} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} f(2,3,z)=\frac{2+9-2z}{4+9+z^2 }=\frac{11-2z}{13+z^2 } \end{equation*}
