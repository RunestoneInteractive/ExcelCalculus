Example 4.2.7. General derivatives of products.

Find the derivatives of the following functions:

  1. \(f(x)=(6x+100)*(1.06)^x.\)
  2. \(g(x)=\sqrt{x} \ln(x).\)

Solution:

  1. \(f'(x)=(6)*(1.06)^x+(6x+100)*(1.06)^x \ln(1.06)\)
  2. \(g'(x)=[\sqrt{x}]'\ln(x)+\sqrt{x}[\ln(x)]'= [1/(2\sqrt{x})]\ln(x)+\sqrt{x}[1/x].\)
