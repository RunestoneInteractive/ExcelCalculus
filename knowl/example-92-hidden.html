Find the discriminant of

\begin{equation*} f(x,y)=x^2+ 3xy+5y^3-7x-11y. \end{equation*}

Solution: We have already computed the second partial derivatives.

\begin{equation*} f_xx=2,\quad f_xy=3,\quad f_yy=30y. \end{equation*}

Substituting into the formula

\begin{equation*} D=(2)(30y)-3^2=60y-9. \end{equation*}
