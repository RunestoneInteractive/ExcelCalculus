Item 7.8.1.9.a.

The two curve intersect at the point of market equilibrium, \((10, 110)\text{.}\)

\begin{equation*} ProdSurplus= \int_0^{10} (110)- (10+q^2)\ dx=\frac{2000}{3}. \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} ConsSurplus= \int_0^{10} (210-q^2)-110\ dx=\frac{2000}{3}. \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} TotalSocialGain=ProdSurplus+ConsSurplus=\frac{4000}{3}. \end{equation*}
