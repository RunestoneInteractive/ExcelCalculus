Item.

We can maximize revenue by finding the price and quantity when \(E = 1\) (unit elastic). We want

\begin{equation*} 1=E=\frac{-1}{dP/dQ}\frac{P(q)}{q} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} 1=50 \frac{30-q/50}{q}=\frac{1500-q}{q} \end{equation*}

First solve for \(q\text{:}\)

\begin{equation*} q=1500-q \Longrightarrow 2q=1500 \Longrightarrow q=750 \end{equation*}

Next find the price:

\begin{equation*} price=30-\frac{quantity}{50},\qquad price(750=30-\frac{750}{50}=30-15=15 \end{equation*}

Hence we have a maximum revenue when \(quantity=750\) and \(price=\)$15. (The actual maximum revenue is \(revenue=quantity*price=750*15=\)$11,250.)
