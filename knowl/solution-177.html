Solution 5.2.1.7.1.

First find the derivative

\begin{equation*} \frac{dz}{dy}= \frac{dz/dx}{dy/dx}=\frac{50}{1000*1.05^x*\ln(1.05)} \end{equation*}

Next plug in our value of \(x\text{:}\)

\begin{equation*} \text{When }x=10, \frac{dz}{dy}=\frac{50}{1000*1.05^{10}*\ln(1.05)} \end{equation*}
