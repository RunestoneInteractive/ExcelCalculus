Solution 4.4.5.1.

Wolfram writes ln as log but still means base \(e\text{.}\) the common log of x would be log(10,x). To stick with our notation we would say

\begin{equation*} m'(x)=\frac{2 x}{(x^2+3)ln(x^2+3)ln(ln(x^2+3))} \end{equation*}
