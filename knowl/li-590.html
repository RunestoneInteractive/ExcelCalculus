Item.

We can maximize revenue by finding the price and quantity when \(E = 1\) (unit elastic). We want

\begin{equation*} 1=E=\frac{-1}{dP/dQ}\frac{P(q)}{q} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} 1=-\frac{1}{20*(.95)^{q/50}\ln(0.95)\frac{1}{50}} \frac{20*(.95)^{q/50}}{q} =-\frac{50}{q ln(0.95)} \end{equation*}

And hence \(\ln(.95) q= -50\text{,}\) so \(q= -50/\ln(.95)\approx 975\text{.}\) When \(q=975\text{,}\) we have that \(price=20*(.95)^{(975/50) }=\$7.36\text{.}\)
