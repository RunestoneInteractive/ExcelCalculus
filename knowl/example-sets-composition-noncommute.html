Example5.2.11

Let \(f(x) = x^2\) and \(g(x) = 2x + 5\). Then \begin{equation*}(f \circ g)(x) = f(g(x)) = (2x + 5)^2 = 4x^2 + 20x + 25\end{equation*} and \begin{equation*}(g \circ f)(x) = g(f(x)) = 2x^2 + 5.\end{equation*} In general, order makes a difference; that is, in most cases \(f \circ g \neq g \circ f\).
